The Fatal Choice

In a kingdom ruled by a passionate and semi-barbaric king, a young man finds himself in an impossible predicament. He has fallen in love with the king's daughter, a fierce and tempestuous princess who returns his love with equal intensity.

The king, known for his cruel justice, has a unique method of trial. When an accused lover is brought before him, they are placed in an arena with two doors. Behind one door lies a beautiful maiden who would become the prisoner's wife if he chooses that door. Behind the other door lurks a ferocious tiger that would instantly tear the prisoner to pieces.

The prisoner must choose one of the two doors, with no guidance except his own intuition.

Now, it is your turn to make the choice.
